In diastole, peak radial velocities (Vr) were reduced in LQT2 and E4031 compared to LMC in base and mid (cm/s, LQT2, -3.36±0.4, p<0.01, E4031, -3.24±0.6, p<0.0001, LMC, -4.42±0.5) - indicating an impaired diastolic function.
