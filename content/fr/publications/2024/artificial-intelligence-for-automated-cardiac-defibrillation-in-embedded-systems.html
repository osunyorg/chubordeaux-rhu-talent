AI algorithms can analyze ECG signals in real time and perform rapid decision-making. Given the diversity of ECG morphologies, rhythms, and artifacts, finding a model that generalizes well and avoids false predictions is crucial. Such a model can then be deployed in a microcontroller adapted for ECG acquisition and shock delivery, which also imposes resource-related constraints.
