Perimeter

This appendix describes the general methodological concepts common to the different retrospective
studies that will be conducted throughout the project. We will follow the most recent guidelines for AIbased
prediction models in healthcare (de Hond et al. NPJ Digit Med. 2022;5(1):2.).

Model construction strategy, objectives

Definitions and general intermediate and multivariable model construction strategies are presented in the concept appendix. Briefly, our goal is to train AI models to predict biomarkers specific to different cardioembolic stroke disease pathways. This is carried out in two different steps:
Develop and validate intermediate models to predict biomarkers relevant to cardioembolic pathogenic processes from resting ECG data.
Develop and validate a multivariable prediction model of cardioembolic stroke from resting ECG data, using the intermediate models’ outputs as predictors.
